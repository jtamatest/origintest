ABOUT

Welcome to The Origin Thai, where our passion is to introduce the Smithfield community to the bold and diverse flavors of Thai cuisine. Our culinary team, led by a seasoned female chef, expertly combines traditional recipes with innovative flair.

Complementing our dining experience, our experienced female mixologist has crafted unique cocktails in a lively bar setting. At The Origin Thai, we strive to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for intimate meals or social gatherings. Our goal is to become your go-to destination, a place where you'll always feel at home.
